How SAT Scores Are Calculated

  • Raw Score: Number of questions you got right (no penalty for wrong answers)
  • Section Score: Raw score converted to 200–800 scale using College Board tables
  • Total Score: Math + Reading/Writing = 400–1600
  • Percentile: How you rank nationally (based on recent test data)
