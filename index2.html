Frequently Asked Questions

Are CalcVibe calculators free to use?

Yes, all calculators on CalcVibe are 100% free. No signup, no payment, no usage limits. Use as many times as you need for academic, professional, or gaming purposes.

How accurate are the AP score calculators?

Our AP calculators (APUSH, AP Biology, AP Chemistry, etc.) use the official 2025 College Board scoring guidelines and rubrics. They provide highly accurate estimates based on raw scores and performance metrics. Results are for estimation only and should not replace official scores.

Do you support weighted GPA with AP/IB classes?

Yes, our GPA Calculator supports weighted grading scales including AP/IB (+1.0), Honors (+0.5), and custom scales. You can add as many courses as needed with individual credit hours and grades.

Are Pokémon calculators up to date with Gen 9?

All Pokémon tools (Type Calculator, Damage Calculator, IV Calculator) use the latest Gen 9 mechanics including Tera Types, STAB, critical hits, and updated type charts. Perfect for Scarlet/Violet competitive play.

Can I use construction calculators for real projects?

Yes! Topsoil, Mulch, and Gravel calculators use standard landscaping formulas. Input your area and depth to get precise volume in cubic yards or meters. Ideal for gardening, lawn care, and construction planning.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. CalcVibe does not store any user data. All calculations happen in your browser. No cookies, no tracking, no personal information required. Your privacy is our priority.
