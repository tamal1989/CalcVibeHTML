About the Topsoil Calculator

This calculator estimates the amount of topsoil needed for your landscaping project. Enter the area (length × width for rectangular, or diameter for circular) and desired depth to get volume in cubic yards or meters. Includes compaction factor and cost estimate.

Standard depth: 4–6 inches for lawns, 8–12 inches for gardens. Topsoil is sold by the cubic yard (27 cubic feet).

How to Use the Topsoil Calculator

  1. Select area shape (rectangle or circle)
  2. Enter length/width or diameter
  3. Input desired depth (4–6" for lawns, 8–12" for gardens)
  4. Choose units and optional cost per cubic yard
  5. Click "Calculate Topsoil Needed"

Note: Volume = Area × Depth. 1 cubic yard = 27 cubic feet = ~13.5 bags (2 cu ft each).

Topsoil Tips

  • Depth: 4–6 inches for existing lawns, 8–12 inches for new gardens
  • Compaction: Topsoil settles 10–20% — order extra
  • Quality: Look for screened topsoil with organic matter
  • Delivery: Most suppliers deliver by the cubic yard
