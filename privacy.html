Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. CalcVibe does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

Last updated: November 07, 2025

1. No Data Collection

CalcVibe does not collect any personal information. When you use our calculators:

  • No name, email, or contact details are requested.
  • No IP address, location, or device data is logged.
  • All inputs (grades, Pokémon stats, dimensions, etc.) are processed only in your browser.
  • Nothing is sent to our servers.

2. Local Calculations

Every calculator runs 100% client-side. Your data never leaves your device. This means:

  • Instant results without server delays.
  • No risk of data breaches or leaks.
  • You can use CalcVibe offline (after first load).

3. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, local storage, or tracking pixels to monitor your activity.

  • No behavioral tracking.
  • No third-party analytics beyond anonymized page views.
  • No retargeting or personalized ads based on calculator use.

4. Third-Party Services

We use only minimal, privacy-respecting third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymized, no personal data. Used only for page visit counts.
  • Google AdSense: May set advertising cookies. You can block these via browser settings or Google Ads Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

CalcVibe is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected, COPPA compliance is not applicable.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Changes will be posted here with the updated date. Continued use of CalcVibe after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
