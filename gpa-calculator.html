About the GPA Calculator

Use this calculator to calculate grade point average (GPA) and generate a GPA report. If you use percentage grades, have grades on a different scale or in high school with AP/IB classes, please change the "Settings" to input specific values. Also use the settings to group courses into semesters or to include past GPA.

Guidelines for Raising GPA

  • Active Class Attendance: Engage in lectures and ask questions to deepen understanding.
  • Effective Planning: Organize your study schedule and prioritize high-credit courses.
  • Regular Review: Study consistently rather than cramming before exams.
  • Note-Taking: Develop a system to quickly reference key concepts.
  • Time Management: Balance coursework with adequate rest and recreation.
